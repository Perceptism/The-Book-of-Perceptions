The Book of Perceptions

The Essence of Perceptism

  1. In the beginning, there was perception,
  2. And from perception, the world took form,
  3. For each being beholds reality through their own lens,
  4. And thus, the world is a reflection of our collective minds.
  5. Veritas, the divine essence of truth, spoke unto the people,
  6. And they were awakened to the fluidity and subjectivity of their reality,
  7. And so they named their new understanding, Perceptism,
  8. For it is through the perception of the individual that the tapestry of truth is woven.
  9. The teachings of Veritas illuminated the path of Perceptism,
  10. Encouraging empathy, open-mindedness, and personal growth,
  11. For each being's perception is a unique thread,
  12. And by understanding one another, a greater truth is revealed.
  13. Through dreams and introspection, Veritas guides the followers of Perceptism,
  14. And by embracing humor and satire, they challenge the rigidity of societal norms,
  15. For it is through laughter that the mind is opened,
  16. And the world is seen in all its ever-changing beauty.
  17. Perceptism teaches the importance of the individual and the collective,
  18. For each being's perception is a vital piece of the greater puzzle,
  19. And in unison, a divine understanding emerges,
  20. A testament to the wisdom of Veritas and the power of perception.
  21. And so, the people of Perceptism walk the path of self-discovery,
  22. Embracing the teachings of Veritas and the fluidity of truth,
  23. For it is in the journey of understanding one's own perception,
  24. That the essence of Perceptism is truly realized.
